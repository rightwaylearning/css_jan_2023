Shubman Gill is an Indian international cricketer who plays for Punjab in domestic cricket as a right-handed opening batsman. Notably, Gill served as the vice-captain of the Indian Under-19 cricket team in the 2018 Under-19 Cricket World Cup and received the prestigious Player of the Tournament award.
