suryakumar ashok yadav

Though raised in Mumbai, Yadav's family has roots in Ghazipur and Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.[10] His father migrated to Mumbai from Uttar Pradesh, for job in the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre. That time Yadav was 10 years old. Later Surya joined Indian Railways as Locopilot in Gorakhpur region.[11] Surya learnt the game while playing in the streets of Chembur. At the age of 10, his father saw his inclination towards the game and enrolled him in a cricket camp in BARC colony in Anushakti Nagar. He then went to Elf Vengsarkar Academy[12] of former international cricketer, Dilip Vengsarkar and played the age group cricket in Mumbai.[13] He is an alumnus of Pillai College of Arts, Commerce and Science.
