Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the Indian cricket team. Considered as one of the best opening batters of all time, Rohit is known for his timing, elegance, six-hiting abilities and leadership skills. Wikipedia

Hardik Himanshu Pandya is an Indian international cricketer. An All-rounder who bats right-handed and bowls right-arm fast-medium, Pandya has played in all 3 formats for India. Wikipedia
